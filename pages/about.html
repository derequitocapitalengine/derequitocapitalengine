About the Engine

Built to turn investment discipline into a repeatable system.

The Derequito Capital Engine™ is a proprietary rules-based investment operating system created to bring structure, consistency, and long-term discipline to portfolio decision-making.

The Purpose

Investing principles are valuable. A working process makes them usable.

Long-term investors are often told to remain patient, diversified, disciplined, and focused on business quality. Those principles are sound, but they do not always answer the practical questions that arise when real capital must be managed.

What should receive new capital? How large should a position become? When should an investor hold, add, trim, rebalance, or reassess the original business thesis?

The Derequito Capital Engine™ was created to translate those recurring decisions into a structured operating framework.

The Problem

Emotion enters investing when the process is unclear.

Without a defined process, investors can become overly influenced by market noise, recent price movement, fear, excitement, or changing personal conviction.

Even a strong long-term philosophy can become inconsistent when allocation, position sizing, portfolio health, and review standards are handled differently each time.

The engine is designed to reduce that inconsistency by making decision quality more deliberate, documented, and repeatable.

What It Is Not

The engine is not a stock‑picking promise or a short‑term trading signal.

The Derequito Capital Engine™ does not guarantee returns, eliminate investment risk, or replace independent judgment.

It does not attempt to predict every market movement. Its purpose is to improve the consistency and quality of the decisions made within a long-term investment process.

The system supports discipline. Responsibility for every investment decision remains with the investor.

The Founder

Built from personal struggle, then shaped for the next generation.

The Derequito Capital Engine™ began with the founder’s own struggles with trades, prices, entries, allocation, and the emotional pressure of making investment decisions without a complete operating process.

It was built so his children would not have to begin with the same uncertainty. His daughter is already learning the discipline behind the system, including how prices, rules, and long-term decisions work when the process is followed.

His son may also become a future end user as he grows older. The long-term purpose is larger than one portfolio: to create a system that can be understood, maintained, and responsibly passed forward.

That same principle may extend to every investor and family who uses it—turning hard-earned experience into a clearer path for the next generation.
